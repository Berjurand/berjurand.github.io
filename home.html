welcome to Drake Hash House Harriers home on the web.

We're a small club in the south west of England dedicated to hashing, a sociable non-competitive sport, in which runners follow a pre-laid trail across the countryside. The aim is for people of all abilities to run together and so every trail has shortcuts, loops and false trails built in. We meet every Monday evening at 7.30pm, and as the emphasis is on having a good time, we always go to a local pub after each run.

All a hasher needs are running shoes and a sense of humour. Fitness is definitely not a requirement, which is just as well, because most hashers are fit for nothing anyway!

Please look around our site to find out more about us and hashing. Click the links to find a hash club near you.
