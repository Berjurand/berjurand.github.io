DRAKE'S COUNTRY 1: FROM SPACE TO SHEEPSTOR VILLAGE


Drake H3's country is in the south west of England, taking in the area from Dartmoor to the south Devon coast. Dartmoor is an area of high, open moorland with unusual rocky outcrops called tors. It gets a lot of rain, which means there are many fast flowing rivers and streams, as well as extensive bogs. Once the rivers leave the moor their valleys become heavily wooded, and the surrounding countryside changes to rich pastureland. The south Devon coast is very beautiful with high rocky cliffs and attractive coves and beaches. The river estuaries, which are wide and shallow, are penetrated far upstream by the tide. This is because they are rias, drowned river valleys, which came about because of an increase in sea level at the end of the last Ice Age. It is well known that of all the types of rias you can get, what is termed the 'dire ria' is the worst.
