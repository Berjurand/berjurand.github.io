Snowdonia weekend Friday 24 to Sunday 26 November 2000
You, the undersigned, have voluntarily volunteered to go, on a purely voluntary basis as
volunteers on the Drake Tours Snowdonia weekend:
No Handle, Squawk, Son of Squawk (SOS), Donkey, Dishy, Mole, Limpalong, Plodder, Anonymouse, The Beast, Twice A Week + One Spare Place.

Accommodation is at the old alma mater, The Alpine Lodge Hotel, Llanberis, consisting of their excessively comfortable bunkroom for 8 and the exclusive cabin for 4. There is a

In order to secure this provisional booking The Beast needs a deposit of £12.50 per
person as soon as possible .The usual chaotic arrangements regarding transport apply and you can arrive as late as you like on Friday night, though preferably before closing time. Timetable: Arrive Friday night, leave Sunday afternoon - simple really. Absolutely no discount if you bring your own chandalier.
